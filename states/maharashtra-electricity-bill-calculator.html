How to Calculate Your Bill With Subsidy

Example: 250 Units Consumed (Monthly)

  1. First 100 units: 100 units x ₹5.50 = ₹550
  2. Next 150 units (101-250): 150 units x ₹10.05 = ₹1507.50
  3. Total Energy Charge: ₹550 + ₹1507.50 = ₹2057.50
  4. Add Fixed Charges: ₹110
  5. Add FAC (example): Assuming FAC of ₹1.35/unit: 250 x ₹1.35 = ₹337.50
  6. Final Payable Amount: ₹2057.50 + ₹110 + ₹337.50 = ₹2505 (Approx.)

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does 1 unit of electricity cost in Maharashtra?

For domestic MSEDCL consumers in Maharashtra, the cost per unit is slab-based. For example, for 0-100 units, the rate is ₹5.50/unit, while for 101-300 units, it is ₹10.05/unit. A Fuel Adjustment Charge (FAC) is also added.

How is the MSEDCL/Mahavitaran electricity bill calculated?

The MSEDCL bill is calculated based on monthly consumption slabs. The energy charge is determined by these slabs, and then fixed charges (which vary by consumption) and the Fuel Adjustment Charge (FAC) are added to get the total amount.

What subsidy is available for electricity in Maharashtra?

The Maharashtra government provides a direct subsidy to MSEDCL to keep the tariff rates for consumers lower than the actual cost of supply. This is reflected in the final tariff rates set by the regulatory commission, MERC.

What is the electricity billing cycle in Maharashtra?

The electricity billing cycle for most domestic consumers in Maharashtra is monthly.

Energy Saving Tips for Maharashtra

Beat the Monsoon Humidity

During the humid monsoon season, use the 'dry' or 'dehumidifier' mode on your AC. It consumes significantly less power than the standard 'cool' mode while providing comfort.

Switch to BEE 5-Star Appliances

In cities like Mumbai and Pune, where appliance usage is high, upgrading to BEE 5-star rated refrigerators, ACs, and washing machines can lead to substantial monthly savings.

Use Geysers Wisely in Winter

In winter, heat water only when needed and switch off the geyser immediately after use. Insulating the hot water pipe can also help reduce heat loss and save energy.
